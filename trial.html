Developed an e-commerce app where teachers can shop online for class assignments,
worksheets, coloring activities, posters and curriculums based on the common core state
standards. The state standards are pulled from CCSS API. Teachers are also allowed to become
a seller to create and sell their own teaching materials. Teachers can reach out a variety of
teaching materials created and used by other fellow teachers, add their items to the shop cart.
They are able to make payment through Stripe Api.
